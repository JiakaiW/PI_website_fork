Highly Stable CNOT Gate for Fluxonium Qubits

My research focuses on developing gates for quantum information processing using fluxonium qubits. We have achieved a gate fidelity exceeding 99.9% and demonstrated stability for 24 days run without any need for recalibration. This exceptional stability indicates minimal decoherence-related errors during gate operations, which is crucial for building more reliable and scalable quantum circuits.

Key Findings

  • Achieved CNOT gate fidelity exceeding 99.9%.
  • Demonstrated 24-day gate stability without recalibration.
  • Showed minimal decoherence-related errors during gate operation.
  • Potential for more reliable and scalable quantum circuits.
